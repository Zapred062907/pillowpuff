About Us

Welcome to our product website! We are Grade 12 Gates ICT students from Benigno S. Aquino National High School, and this website is part of our journey into entrepreneurship. For our project, we chose to create a unique, modern brand around a delicious Japanese dessert mochi! Our goal is to bring a variety of soft, flavorful mochi to satisfy every taste, from chocolate and vanilla to matcha and caramel. Through Pillow Puff Mochi, we aim to learn about product development, branding, and connecting with customers. We hope you enjoy exploring our website and discovering our passion for making something special!
